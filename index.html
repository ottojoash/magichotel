Frequently Asked Questions

What are the check-in and check-out times? Rooms ▼
Check-in is from 2:00 PM, and check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out are available upon request, subject to availability.
Do you offer room service 24/7? Rooms ▼
Yes, our room service is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Enjoy a wide selection of meals and beverages delivered directly to your room.
Can I request an extra bed or crib? Rooms ▼
Absolutely! Extra beds and cribs are available upon request. Please notify us during booking or at check-in.
Do I need to book spa treatments in advance? Spa ▼
Yes, we highly recommend booking your spa treatments at least 24 hours in advance to secure your preferred time slot.
What spa services do you offer? Spa ▼
We offer a wide range of services including Swedish massage, deep tissue massage, facials, body wraps, aromatherapy, and couple's treatments.
Is there a dress code for the spa? Spa ▼
We provide robes and slippers for all spa guests. You may wear a swimsuit or use our disposable undergarments for treatments.
Is the gym open 24 hours? Gym ▼
Yes, our fitness center is open 24/7 for hotel guests. Personal trainers are available during staffed hours from 6 AM to 10 PM.
Do you have personal trainers available? Gym ▼
Yes, certified personal trainers are available for one-on-one sessions. Book at the front desk or call the gym reception.
What equipment does the gym have? Gym ▼
Our gym features treadmills, ellipticals, stationary bikes, free weights, resistance machines, and a dedicated yoga/stretching area.
Do you accommodate dietary restrictions? Restaurant ▼
Absolutely! We cater to vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, and other dietary needs. Please inform our staff of any requirements.
Do I need a reservation for dinner? Restaurant ▼
Reservations are recommended for dinner, especially on weekends. Walk-ins are welcome based on availability.
What are your restaurant hours? Restaurant ▼
Breakfast: 6:30 AM - 10:30 AM | Lunch: 12:00 PM - 3:00 PM | Dinner: 6:00 PM - 11:00 PM
What drinks do you serve at the bar? Bar ▼
We offer a wide selection of cocktails, wines, beers, spirits, and non-alcoholic beverages. Our signature cocktail menu changes seasonally.
Do you have happy hour specials? Bar ▼
Yes! Happy hour is from 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM daily with selected cocktails and beers at special prices.
Can I host a private event at the bar? Bar ▼
Yes, our bar area can be reserved for private parties and events. Please contact our events team for more information.
Do you serve breakfast buffet? Restaurant ▼
Yes, we offer a lavish breakfast buffet every morning featuring international and local cuisine.
